Understanding the Futures Prop Firm Comparison Table

All-In Price

Total cost to get started — evaluation fee plus any activation fee. Reflects the discounted price when using code SOPF.

Profit Target

Amount of profit you must reach during evaluation to qualify for a funded account.

Drawdown

End-of-Day updates after close. Intraday tracks unrealized P&L live — most aggressive. Static never moves.

P/D Ratio

Profit Target ÷ Drawdown. Lower ratio (closer to 1.0) = easier eval. Higher = harder to pass.

Daily Loss Limit

Max you can lose in one session. Soft breach locks you out for the day. Hard breach fails the account.

Days to Pass

Minimum trading days required before your eval can be approved.

Days to $

How quickly you can request your first payout after passing. Ranges from 1 to 10+ days.

Profit Split

Your percentage of profits on each payout. Most firms offer 80–90%. Some give 100% on the first tier.

Max Funded

How many simultaneous funded accounts you can hold. Higher = more scaling potential.
