Understanding the Futures Prop Firm Comparison Table

All-In Price

Total cost — eval fee plus any activation fee. Reflects the discounted price when using code SOPF.

Profit Target

Amount you must earn during evaluation to qualify for a funded account.

Drawdown

End-of-Day updates after close. Intraday trails unrealized P&L live. Static never moves.

P/D Ratio

Profit Target ÷ Drawdown. Lower = easier eval. Green ≤1.0, gray ≤1.5, amber >1.5.

Daily Loss Limit

Max per session. Soft breach locks you out. Hard breach fails the account. $0 = no limit.

Days to Pass

Minimum trading days required before your eval can be approved.

Days to $

How quickly you can request your first payout. Ranges from 1 to 10+ days.

Profit Split

Your share of profits on each payout. Most firms offer 80–90%. Some give 100% on the first tier.

Max Funded

Simultaneous funded accounts allowed. Higher = more scaling potential.
